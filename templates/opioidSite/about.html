The opioid crisis is devastating communities across America. The latest statistics indicate that the total yearly overdose deaths due to opioids reached a new high of 70,237 in 2017. In October 2017, President Trump declared the opioid crisis a public health emergency.

This national crisis is alarming as the number of opioid-related overdose deaths continue to climb. This is affecting everyone nationwide: friends, cousins, siblings, parents and children. These are people.

Several measures have been taken to combat the crisis. However, there are few options for accessible data that exist to allow government officials and prescribers to simply view the volume of prescriptions that are taking place overall. This application fills that hole in internet resources.
